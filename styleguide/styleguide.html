Introduction

Hello! Welcome to the design system for Oregon Digital. Oregon Digital is a collaboration between Oregon State University and University of Oregon. While each institution has their own brands at communication.uoregon.edu and communications.oregonstate.edu, this design system is situated to complement each of those as an expression of a collaboration.

Please feel free to collaborate with the project on Github.

Paragraphs

The collections of Oregon Digital are primarily created to support the teaching and research missions of the University of Oregon and Oregon State University. The collections are comprised of unique digitized and born-digital materials including photographs, articles, sheet music, manuscripts, ephemera, and more. Oregon Digital is collaboratively managed by the Digital Scholarship Center of the University of Oregon Libraries and by Oregon State University Libraries.
